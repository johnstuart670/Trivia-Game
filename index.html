How to Calculate Net Carbs

Net Carbs are calculated by subtracting the grams of fiber from the total grams of carbohydrates. In the above image, you can see that the Total Carbohydrates totals "37g" while the Dietary Fiber is "4g". If we follow the formula, we find that the Net Carbs of this example is 33g.

Total Carbohydrates (37g) - Dietary Fiber (4g) = Net Carbs (33g)
